PM-KISAN

Direct cash support of ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers

✅ ₹2.81 lakh crore distributed
✅ 11+ crore beneficiaries
✅ ₹2,000 in 3 equal installments
✅ Directly to bank account via DBT

Per Drop More Crop (PDMC)

Promotes micro-irrigation like drip and sprinkler systems

✅ 78 lakh hectares covered till 2022-23
✅ 20-48% water saving
✅ 30-50% increase in productivity
✅ 55% subsidy for small farmers
2018-19

✅ 3% interest subvention
✅ 5 years moratorium period
✅ 7 years repayment period
✅ Loans available to state governments
